How to Test

  1. Open the main application (index.html)
  2. Click the "Community" button to browse community presets
  3. Use search and category filters to find specific presets
  4. Click "Load" to apply a preset to your current session
  5. Click "Share" to access sharing options
  6. Try uploading your own preset using the upload form
